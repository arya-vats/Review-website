Spies in Disguise was written by Brad Copeland and Lloyd Taylor from a screen story by Cindy Davis, inspired by the animated short film Pigeon Impossible by Lucas Martell.



Creators:

Brad Copeland


Stars:

Will Smith, Tom Holland, Karen Gillan


StoryLine

Super spy Lance Sterling and scientist Walter Beckett are almost exact opposites. Lance is smooth, suave and debonair. Walter is not. But what Walter lacks in social skills he makes up for in smarts and invention, creating the awesome gadgets Lance uses on his epic missions.."



The crew follows the employees around 24/7 and captures their quite humorous and bizarre encounters as they will do what it takes to keep the company thriving.


Trivia

The film is inspired by Lucas Martell's animated short Pigeon: Impossible (2009). Will Smith's first animated film since Shark Tale (2004), released fifteen years prior. Agent Sterling has a tattoo of the US. Marine Corps Emblem of the Eagle, Globe, and Anchor on the left side of his chest (above his heart). underwear.
