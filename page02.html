Based on the life of the Israeli spy, Eli Cohen.


Creators:

Gideon Raff


Stars:

Sacha Baron Cohen, Hadar Ratzon Rotem, Yael Eitan


StoryLine

The miniseries follows the exploits of Eli Cohen, a Mossad spy. While Israel prepares to strike a Syrian target identified by Eli, suspicion closes in around him. Retaliation is swift -- and ruthless.



Trivia


Sacha Baron Cohen is 6'3" tall compared to Eli Cohen's diminutive 5'6".
