Money Heist (Spanish: La casa de papel) is a Spanish heist crime drama television series created by Álex Pina.


Creators:

Álex Pina


Stars:

Úrsula Corberó, Álvaro Morte, Itziar Ituño


StoryLine

An unusual group of robbers attempt to carry out the most perfect robbery in
Spanish history - stealing 2.4 billion euros from the Royal Mint of Spain.



Trivia


This Spanish TV series is the most watched non-English show on Netflix since its release
