A low-level Cabinet member becomes President of the United States after a catastrophic attack kills everyone above him in the line of succession.



Creators:

David Guggenheim


Stars:

Kiefer Sutherland, Adan Canto, Italia Ricci


StoryLine

On the night of the State of the Union, an explosion destroys the Capitol Building, killing the President and everyone in the line of succession except for Secretary of Housing and Urban Development
