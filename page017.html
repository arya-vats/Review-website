The series follows a group of people whose plane is hijacked while they are on board a red-eye flight from Brussels.



Creators:

Jason George


Stars:

Pauline Etienne, Laurent Capelluto, Mehmet Kurtulus


StoryLine

When radiation from the sun causes a global disaster on Earth, survivors on an overnight flight from Brussels race from city to city trying to stay ahead of the sun's rays by remaining in the cover of night.



Trivia


The series is inspired by the 2015 Polish science fiction novel The Old Axolotl by Jacek Dukaj.
