A chronicled look at the criminal exploits of Colombian drug lord Pablo Escobar, as well as the many other drug kingpins who plagued the country through the years.



Creators:

Pedro Pascal, Wagner Moura, Boyd Holbrook |


Stars:

Carlo Bernard, Chris Brancato, Doug Miro


StoryLine

The rise of the Guadalajara Cartel as an American DEA agent learns the danger of targeting narcos in Mexico.



Trivia


Season 1 and 2 take place between 1979-93 with Season 3 picking up in 1994.
